badcommunication online can cause problems. For example, if someone sends an email without proper greeting or writes in a rude or casual way, it can create a bad impression.

Task 5 – Cybercrime Awareness
Case Study
Phishing is a very common cybercrime. In this, scammers try to trick people into giving their personal or bank details.
For example, a student may receive a message saying their bank account will be blocked unless they verify it. The message includes a link. When the student clicks it, it opens a fake website that looks real. If the student enters their details or OTP, the scammer gets access.
Many students fall for this because the message looks urgent and real. After that, money can be stolen or accounts can be misused.
This shows how important it is to stay alert while using the internet.
